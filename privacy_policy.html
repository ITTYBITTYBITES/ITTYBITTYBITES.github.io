Privacy Policy

The 2-Second Witness

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Introduction

Itty Bitty Bites ("we", "us", "our") operates The 2-Second Witness mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Data We Collect

The 2-Second Witness does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game progress is stored locally on your device only. We do not require account creation or sign-in.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob - for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Ads

We display banner ads on menu screens and offer optional rewarded video ads. You are never required to watch an ad to play the game. Premium users ("Director's Access") see no ads.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Storage

All game data (scores, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to any server. If you uninstall the app, all local data is deleted.

In-App Purchases

The app offers an optional one-time purchase ("Director's Access") processed through Google Play. We do not handle or store any payment information. All payment processing is handled by Google Play.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
