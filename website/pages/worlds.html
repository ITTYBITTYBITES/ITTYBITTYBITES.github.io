BIOMES & ERAS

Witness The Worlds

Every biome presents a radically distinct visual palette and clutter profile. When a scene flashes for two seconds, your brain must separate environmental noise from crucial changing anomalies.

Core Game Biomes

🕍 Biome 01 • Demo Active

Ancient Egypt

Sun-drenched sandstone corridors, flicking torchlight, and intricate hieroglyphic wall carvings. Notice subtle shifts in ceremonial artifacts, missing scarabs, or altered shadows across pharaonic monuments before the sands shift.

Launch Pharaoh Trial ▶
Difficulty: Tier I Lighting: Golden Hour / Torch
⚔ Biome 02 • Demo Active

Vikings

Mist-shrouded fjords, longship docks, and timber mead halls lined with shields. Track weapon placements, runic carvings on wooden pillars, and raven flight paths amidst freezing snowfall and dense maritime fog.

Launch Longhall Trial ▶
Difficulty: Tier II Lighting: Overcast / Hearth
🚀 Biome 03 • Demo Active

Mars Colony

Pressurized habitats, hydroponic bays, and rust-colored dust storms outside reinforced plexiglass airlocks. Scan digital control consoles, valve states, and robotic rover alignments where technical precision is paramount.

Launch Airlock Trial ▶
Difficulty: Tier III Lighting: Artificial LED / Crimson
🧙 Biome 04 • Phase 4 Target

Fantasy

Arcane libraries, levitating crystal observatories, and enchanted glades. Observe spellbook runes, floating alchemy vials, and shifting magical familiar crests that defy natural geometry during the two-second window.

Difficulty: Tier IV Lighting: Bioluminescent / Arcane
🏠 Biome 05 • Phase 4 Target

Cyberpunk

Rain-slicked megacity alleyways dominated by holographic advertisements, neon noodle bars, and cybernetic drone traffic. High visual sensory overload challenges advanced witnesses to filter intense chromatic noise and pinpoint micro-glitches.

Difficulty: Master Tier V Lighting: Neon / Holographic Daze
