PERCEPTION MODES

Four Cognitive Trials

Two seconds is all you get. Across our game biomes, encounters dynamically rotate between four high-stakes cognitive scenarios designed to push human visual working memory to its absolute limit.

Core Gameplay Scenarios

🧠 Scenario Alpha

Memory Recall

Observe a complex scene for exactly two seconds. Once the scene cuts to black, you face direct interrogative prompts regarding specific objects, colors, quantities, or spatial positions present in the flash.

Cognitive Focus: Short-Term Retention Precision Required
👁 Scenario Beta

Spot Difference

Study Scene A for two seconds. After a brief darkness interval, Scene B appears with subtle environmental alterations—a missing dagger, a rotated statue head, or a shifted console switch. Identify the exact point of divergence.

Cognitive Focus: Micro-Anomaly Detection Speed Tracking
🔢 Scenario Gamma

Sequence Memory

Witness rapid consecutive two-second flashes that show sequential progression across an environment. Reconstruct the precise chronological order of events or trace the trajectory of moving elements.

Cognitive Focus: Temporal Continuity Multi-Frame Tracking
🔮 Scenario Delta

Pattern Recognition

Analyze complex geometric arrays, symbolic glyph structures, or cryptographic neon displays within two seconds. Detect underlying visual rules, symmetry breaks, or hidden repeating motifs before time expires.

Cognitive Focus: Abstract Synthesis Logic & Synthesis

Phase 3 System Operational

The interactive demo engine above runs natively within our zero-dependency static web architecture. Each scenario mode utilizes shared UI feedback loops and responsive 2000ms timing controllers.
