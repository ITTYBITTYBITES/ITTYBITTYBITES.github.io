What Is Change Blindness?

Published on July 5, 2026 • Reading Time: 3 min

The Illusion of Continuous Vision

Change blindness occurs when a visual transient—such as a brief screen blackout, eye blink, or camera cut—masks the motion signals that normally alert our brain to environmental changes.

In our Viking Shipyard or Mars Airlock trials, players regularly fail to notice massive color mutations or 90-degree valve rotations because their visual attention was locked onto neighboring distractors.
