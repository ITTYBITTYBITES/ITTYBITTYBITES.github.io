The Science Behind Visual Attention

Published on July 5, 2026 • Reading Time: 3 min

Foveal vs. Peripheral Gaze

Only the central 2 degrees of our visual field (the fovea) renders high-acuity detail. During a two-second window, the eye makes roughly 6 rapid saccades.
