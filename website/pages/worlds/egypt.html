Ancient Egypt Biome

Sun-drenched sandstone corridors, flickering torchlight, and sacred hieroglyphic chambers. The Egyptian biome tests foundational visual attention by challenging players to spot shifting ceremonial relics and shadow discrepancies.

Cognitive Science & Visual Clutter

In cognitive psychology, high-contrast gold and turquoise elements naturally draw the foveal gaze. In our Ancient Egypt trials, we design visual layouts that deliberately separate high-contrast distractors from subtle background anomalies, forcing players to train their peripheral visual attention.
