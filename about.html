About Two Second Witness

A cognitive instrument, not a game. A living universe, not a content library.

Project Vision

Two Second Witness is an interactive observation discovery experience. You are shown a stimulus for two seconds. You recognize, you respond, you move on. There are no scores judging your intelligence, no timers pressuring your performance, no lives, no levels, no monetization gates disguised as difficulty.

Instead: a spatial cognitive field that grows around your attention. Worlds cluster by what you notice. Characters represent behavioral archetypes derived from real observation mechanics – not website personas.

The app curates a focused, active experience – currently 20 worlds at a time. The Chronicle website preserves the full archive – every world, every observation, every version – searchable forever.

Cognitive Instrument

Observation Engine – Each observation presents a visual/physical signature with deliberate confusions (adjacent principles, rendering artifacts). Difficulty is calibrated, confidence is tracked, but never shown as a competitive score.

Behavioral Fields – Four observer archetypes: Memory Cascade, Rapid Classification, Signal vs Noise, Stroop Test – derived directly from app scenario mechanics, not invented for marketing.

Spatial Field – Worlds exist in a 3D cognitive field with density, intensity, gravity, drift_potential, overlap_regions – rendered in the Chronicle Field visualizer.

Evolution Intelligence – Ranking decides popularity. Placement decides location. Never combined. Lifecycle: active → cooling → archived → resurrectable. Growth proposals are scored with evidence, approved by humans, queued deterministically. The system cannot generate content autonomously.

Accessibility & Privacy

  • No accounts required to explore the Chronicle website
  • No tracking pixels, no analytics cookies, no ad networks on chronicle pages
  • Neutral language – observations are framed cognitively, never medically or diagnostically
  • Color contrast meets WCAG AA – dark theme with high-contrast cyan accents
  • Keyboard navigable – all interactive elements reachable
  • Screen reader friendly – semantic HTML, alt text, ARIA labels where needed
  • Mobile first – responsive layout, touch targets ≥44px
  • Open data – all world/universe/evolution data available as JSON at /data/
  • Deterministic builds – every page regenerates from shared canonical truth – no manual edits
