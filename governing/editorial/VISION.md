# ITTYBITTYBITES Vision

**Status**: Authoritative North Star  
**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Date**: 2026-07-08  

---

## The Why: Our Reason for Being

ITTYBITTYBITES exists to solve a fundamental problem of the digital age: **the exhaustion of the infinite.**

Most of the internet is designed to extract attention through friction, complexity, and endless loops. We believe there is a profound, unmet need for the opposite: **digital spaces that respect the person.**

We build "bites" because the smallest unit of meaning is often the most durable. We build "collections" because context creates value. We build the "platform" because a home for meaningful ideas should be as stable as the ideas themselves.

Our goal is not to keep people on their screens. Our goal is to give them something valuable enough that they are happy to have spent the time, and then let them get back to their lives—only to return when they are ready to discover something new.

---

## Five-Year Success

In five years, ITTYBITTYBITES will be:

1.  **A Trusted Library**: A globally recognized destination for high-quality, interactive understanding. When someone wants to grasp a concept in mathematics, history, or science, they check if there’s a "bite" for it.
2.  **A Standard for Human-Centered Design**: An example of how software can be profitable and sustainable without being manipulative or invasive.
3.  **An Extensible Ecosystem**: A platform where the world’s best educators and creators contribute to a shared, high-quality library governed by the same three laws.
4.  **Digitally Archival**: A collection of experiences that still work, still look beautiful, and still provide value years after they were first published.

---

## What Belongs Here

We welcome experiences that:
-   **Encourage Curiosity**: They ask "what if?" and provide the tools to find out.
-   **Reward Attention**: The more you look, the more you find.
-   **Foster Clarity**: They make complex systems understandable through interaction.
-   **Respect Time**: They have a beginning, a middle, and a meaningful conclusion.
-   **Are Self-Contained**: They do one thing exceptionally well.

---

## What Will Never Belong

We explicitly reject:
-   **Attention-Theft**: Infinite scrolls, autoplay, and manipulative notification loops.
-   **Dark Patterns**: Confusing UI designed to trick users into actions.
-   **Content Without Intent**: "Filler" content created solely for SEO or metric-padding.
-   **Invasive Tracking**: Monitoring individuals for the purpose of extraction.
-   **Planned Obsolescence**: Experiences that break because a server went down or a framework shifted.

---

## The Evaluation: The North Star Question

Every new idea—whether it is a feature, a collection, or a single interaction—must answer one question:

> **"Does this leave the person better than it found them?"**

If the answer is "We don't know" or "No, but it improves our metrics," we do not build it.

If the answer is "Yes, because they now understand [X] / have solved [Y] / feel [Z]," then it has a home here.

---

*Build with curiosity. Design with respect. Create things worth returning to.*
