## Purpose

This document defines the platform’s commitment to accessibility and inclusive design.

It will specify:

- Target conformance level (e.g. WCAG 2.2 AA or higher)
- Experience-level accessibility requirements
- Testing and audit processes
- How accessibility interacts with motion, analytics, and monetization
- Ongoing accessibility governance

Accessibility is non-negotiable and must be designed into the Constitution, Blueprint, and Design System.
