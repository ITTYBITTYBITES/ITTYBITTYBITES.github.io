## Purpose

This document defines how the platform will sustain itself without compromising the user experience or the core philosophy.

It will address:

- Allowed monetization models
- Placement and experience rules (non-intrusive by default)
- Affiliate, advertising, premium, and sponsorship guidelines
- Revenue vs. experience trade-off principles
- Transparency and user trust requirements

All monetization must be derived from and consistent with the Product Constitution.
